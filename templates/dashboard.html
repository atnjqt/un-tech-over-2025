UN-Tech-Over Hackathon - OSPOs for Good 2025

Description:

This dashboard provides a visual representation of geospatial data across various countries referenced in the OSM model. You can filter the data by country and location types to customize your view.

Goal:

Building out the `giga-spatial` platform to improve integration for HDX handler / UNHCR handler to include displaced and refugee populations. To get *Ahead of the Storm*, the team aims to demontrate value for UN members and policy makers in identifying risk for children that are displaced or residential.
